How to Place an Order

  1. Enter your personal information: Name, Email, and Phone Number.
  2. Select your preferred product from the menu.
  3. Choose the quantity of the selected product.
  4. Review the total price before confirming.
  5. Optionally, add any special requests in the Additional Notes field.
  6. Click the "Submit Order" button to complete your order.
  7. Wait for an order confirmation message.
